Our Story

We're Building the Future
of Food in Côte d'Ivoire

AgroConnect CI started with a simple observation: farmers were struggling to sell their produce while city families struggled to find affordable, fresh food. Digital technology is our solution.

Our Mission

Bridging the Gap, Digitally

AgroConnect CI bridges the gap between rural producers and urban consumers using digital technology — improving income for farmers while providing affordable fresh food.

🌱 Empower smallholder farmers across Côte d'Ivoire
🛒 Make fresh, local food accessible to every urban household
💼 Create sustainable jobs in delivery, tech, and support
🇨🇮 Strengthen Côte d'Ivoire's food sovereignty and local economy
🌾
"

Fresh food. Fair prices. Empowered farmers.

We believe that every farmer in Côte d'Ivoire deserves access to a fair market — and every family deserves access to fresh, affordable food. Technology is how we make that happen.

Principles

What We Stand For

01
Farmer First
Every decision we make starts with the question: does this help the farmer? Their income, dignity, and independence come first.
02
Radical Transparency
Farmers see exactly how much we earn per sale. Customers see exactly where their food comes from. No hidden fees, no opacity.
03
Local Always
We will never list imported products when a local alternative exists. Our platform is a celebration of Ivorian agriculture.
04
Accessible Tech
Technology should include people, not exclude them. Our tools work on basic smartphones, in French, and even by voice or SMS.
Social Impact

More Than a Marketplace

AgroConnect CI is a social project built inside a business model. Every transaction creates ripple effects across the Ivorian economy.

Social Benefits

👩‍🌾
Women Farmer Empowerment
Over 60% of our registered farmers are women. We offer free onboarding support in local languages.
♻️
Reduced Food Waste
Farmers sell faster, food moves quicker. Estimated 25% reduction in post-harvest waste among our partner farms.
🎓
Youth Employment in Tech
Our platform is built and maintained by young Ivorian engineers and developers, creating careers in the local tech sector.

Economic Benefits

💰
Higher Farmer Income
By cutting middlemen, farmers earn 25–40% more per kilogram of produce sold through our platform.
🇨🇮
Import Substitution
Every local product bought on AgroConnect CI is a product not imported. We strengthen Côte d'Ivoire's food sovereignty.
🛵
Delivery Job Creation
We directly employ and support over 80 local delivery riders across Abidjan, with more coming as we expand.
