Why Choose Us

A Better Way to Buy & Sell

We believe fresh food should be accessible to everyone — and farmers deserve fair pay for their hard work.

🚫

No Middlemen

Farmers sell directly to you. No brokers, no market stalls taking a cut. More money stays with the people who grow your food.

⚡

Fast Delivery

Same-day or next-day delivery across Abidjan. Our network of local riders ensures your fresh produce arrives on time.

📱

Mobile Money

Pay seamlessly with MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money, or cash on delivery. No bank account required.

✅

Verified Farmers

Every seller on our platform is verified by our team. Buy with confidence knowing exactly where your food comes from.

🌿

100% Local

Every product on AgroConnect CI is grown in Côte d'Ivoire. Support local agriculture, reduce imports, eat better.

💼

Creates Jobs

Each delivery creates income for local riders. Each sale empowers a farmer. Every purchase contributes to the Ivorian economy.
