Customer Journey

Order in 4 Easy Steps

You don't need to be tech-savvy. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use AgroConnect CI.

1. Browse & Choose

Open the website or app, search for what you need — tomatoes, plantain, yams. See fresh stock listed with prices and farm location.

2. Add to Cart & Confirm

Select your quantity, choose home delivery or a nearby pickup point, and confirm your order. You'll get a confirmation via SMS.

3. Pay Your Way

Pay with MTN MoMo, Orange Money, or choose cash on delivery. Your payment is held securely until the order is delivered.

4. Receive & Enjoy

A local rider delivers to your door — usually same day or next morning. Rate your experience and the farmer gets paid instantly.

Farmer Journey

Sell Without Leaving Your Farm

Register once, and buyers come to you. No market, no broker, no transportation cost.

1. Register Free

Sign up with your phone number. Our team will verify your identity and help you set up your seller profile in your local language if needed.

2. List Your Products

Take a photo of your produce, enter the quantity and price. Our simple interface is designed for low-data Android phones.

3. Receive Orders

When a customer orders, you get an alert on WhatsApp or SMS. Prepare the order and wait for the rider to come collect it from your farm or a nearby point.

4. Get Paid Instantly

Once delivery is confirmed, money is transferred directly to your MTN or Orange Money account. No waiting, no intermediaries.

Payments

Simple, Secure Payments

We support the payment methods that Ivorians actually use — no bank account required.

MTN Mobile Money
The most used mobile payment service in Côte d'Ivoire. Instant transfer, available 24/7.
Orange Money
Widely used across Abidjan and rural areas. Simple USSD code payment, no internet needed.
Wave CI
Fast digital wallet with zero transfer fees. Popular among younger urban users in Abidjan.
💵
Cash on Delivery
Not comfortable with digital payments yet? Pay the rider in cash when your order arrives at your door.

🔒 All digital transactions are encrypted and processed through BCEAO-compliant gateways.

FAQ

Common Questions

Is registration free for farmers? +
Yes, 100% free. Farmers pay nothing to register or list products. AgroConnect CI earns a small commission (5–8%) only when a sale is completed.
What is the delivery time? +
Orders placed before 12pm are usually delivered the same day in Abidjan. All orders are guaranteed within 48 hours. B2B partners can schedule daily morning deliveries.
What if I receive a bad product? +
We have a quality guarantee. If the product doesn't match its description, contact us within 2 hours of delivery via WhatsApp or phone. We'll arrange a refund or replacement at no extra cost.
Do I need a smartphone to use AgroConnect CI? +
The website works on any phone with a browser. For farmers with basic phones, our support team can help set up listings by phone call or WhatsApp. We also offer SMS notifications for order updates.
How are payments protected? +
Customer payments are held in escrow until the order is confirmed delivered. The farmer is only paid after successful delivery. All transactions use BCEAO-compliant, encrypted gateways.
